Instructions for using Map

The interactive maps indicate site locations of all consented housing applications from 2012 to 2015, delivering over 5 units. Sites delivering larger number of residential units are represented with specific symbols. The map also includes locations of all local services such as schools, GPs and supermarkets.

Click on the housing symbols to find out more about each application site and the closest local services to each application.

Click on the services to find out about the pressure on each service by emerging applications. The routes represent the five housing applications that are most likely to put pressure on each individual service.

Areas of high housing intensification are highlighted as clusters. Click on each cluster to find out more about their cumulative impact in terms of housing numbers and aggregated statistics.
